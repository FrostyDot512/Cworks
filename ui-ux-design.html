// how it works

Our Process

A clear, collaborative workflow from first conversation to delivery.

01

Brief & Discovery

We talk through your goals, users, and existing brand. What problem are we solving? What should this feel like to use?

02

Research & Planning

We study comparable products and user patterns, then plan the information architecture and user flows before touching Figma.

03

Wireframes

Low-fidelity wireframes establish layout and structure. You review and give feedback before we invest time in visual design.

04

Visual Design

We apply your brand identity — color, typography, depth — and turn wireframes into full high-fidelity screens.

05

Prototype & Review

An interactive prototype lets you experience the design as a user would. You give feedback and we work through revision rounds.

06

Handover

We deliver the final organised Figma file with component library, style guide, and exported assets — ready for developers.

// FAQ

Do you design for mobile and desktop?

Yes. All designs are mobile-first, then scaled up to tablet and desktop.

What tools do you use?

We design in Figma. You'll receive a fully organised Figma file with components, styles, and a prototype.

Can you design for an existing brand?

Yes. Share your brand guidelines, colours, and fonts and we'll design within them.

Can the design be handed off to developers?

Yes. The Figma file includes developer-ready specs, exportable assets, and a component library.
