No hidden extras. Here is exactly what every design engagement covers.

Research & Discovery

We start by getting under the skin of your project. Who your users are, what they need, and what your competitors are doing. This shapes every design decision that follows.

Wireframing

Before anything looks polished, we map out the structure. Low-fidelity wireframes define layout, hierarchy, and user flow so we're aligned on the fundamentals before visual design begins.

Every tier includes a set number of revision rounds. We collect your feedback, make the changes, and share the updated design, until you're happy to sign off.

// how it works

Our Process

A clear, collaborative design workflow from first brief to final file.

01

Brief & Discovery

We start with a conversation about your project, your users, and your goals. We ask the right questions to understand not just what you want, but why? So the design solves a real problem.

02

Research & Planning

We review your competitors, define your user personas, and map out the key flows your design needs to support. This gives us a clear blueprint before any design work begins.

03

Wireframes

We produce low-fidelity wireframes that lay out every screen (structure, content hierarchy, and navigation) and share them with you for feedback. Nothing is finalised until you sign off.

04

Visual Design

With the wireframes approved, we apply the full visual layer (colour, type, icons, imagery, and component styling), producing polished, high-fidelity screens in Figma.

05

Prototype & Review

We build an interactive prototype and share it with you for review. You go through the experience as a user would, give feedback, and we work through the agreed revision rounds.

06

Handover

We deliver the final Figma file. Fully organised, with a component library, style guide, and all assets exported and ready. We walk you through everything before we close the project.

// faq

Do you design for mobile and desktop?

Yes. All designs are created with responsive layouts in mind. We design for mobile first, then scale up to tablet and desktop.

What tools do you use?

We design in Figma. You'll receive a fully organised Figma file with components, styles, and a prototype on completion.

Can you design for an existing brand?

Yes. Share your existing brand guidelines, colours, and fonts and we'll design within them. If you don't have brand guidelines yet, we can help establish them.

Can the design be handed off to developers?

Yes. The final Figma file includes developer-ready specs, exportable assets, and a component library. If Cworks is also building the site, handoff is seamless.

How many revision rounds are included?

The number of revision rounds depends on your tier. Additional rounds beyond what's included can be arranged for an extra fee.
