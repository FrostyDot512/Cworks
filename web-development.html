// how it works

Our Process

A clear, collaborative workflow from first conversation to delivery.

01

Discovery Call

We talk through your goals, audience, timeline, and budget. No pressure — just a conversation to see if we're a fit.

02

Proposal & Agreement

We send a clear scope, timeline, and pricing. 50% deposit to start, balance on launch.

03

Design

We design the layout in Figma or directly in-browser. You review and give feedback before we build.

04

Build

Clean HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No bloated frameworks unless needed. We keep you updated throughout.

05

Review & Revisions

You review a staging version. We make revisions until you're satisfied within your included rounds.

06

Launch & Handover

We go live, set up your domain and hosting, and hand over all files and access. You own everything.

// FAQ

How long does a website take to build?

Starter sites take around 1 week. Standard builds take 2–3 weeks. Super sites are scoped per project — timeline agreed upfront.

Do I need to provide the content?

Yes. You provide your text, images, and branding assets. We can advise on structure and sourcing if needed.

Can you update my existing site?

Yes. Share details about your current site and what you'd like changed and we'll assess and quote.

What do I need to get started?

Just a rough idea of what you want, some sites you like the look of, and your budget. We'll handle the rest.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes. Post-launch support is available for updates, fixes, and small changes on a retainer or case-by-case basis.
