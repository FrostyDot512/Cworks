// what you get

Everything You
Need, Included

No hidden extras. Here is exactly what every website build covers.

Design

We start with your goals, brand, and inspiration. From there we put together a visual layout (colours, typography, and structure) and share it with you for feedback before a single line of code is written.

Development

Your site is built in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, no bloat. Every site is built with mobile compatibility, tested across browsers, and optimised for fast load times.

Content Setup

You provide your text and images, and we place and format everything to look its best. We'll let you know exactly what we need and in what format before we get started.

Hosting & Deployment

We handle the full deployment, from setting up your hosting environment to connecting your domain. Starter sites go on GitHub Pages; Standard and Super sites are hosted on our server with a custom domain.

Handover & Support

Once the site is live, you receive the full source code. We walk you through how everything works and stay available for a post-launch support window to catch anything that needs fixing.

Timeline

Timelines are agreed upfront and depend on the tier and project complexity. We keep you updated throughout so there are no surprises.

// how it works

Our Process

A clear, collaborative workflow from first conversation to launch day.

01

Discovery Call

We sit down with you, in person or virtually, to understand what you need. We talk through your goals, your audience, any sites you like the look of, and your budget. This gives us everything we need to put together a solid proposal.

02

Proposal & Agreement

We send you a clear breakdown of scope, cost, and timeline. Once you're happy, we agree on payment terms and get the project officially started. No hidden fees, no surprises.

03

Design

We build out the visual direction of your site (layout, colours, fonts, and structure). You review and give feedback, and we refine until you sign off. Nothing moves to code until the design is approved.

04

Build

With the design locked in, we build your site. We share progress along the way and give you a preview link before the final review so you can see exactly how it looks and feels.

05

Review & Revisions

You go through the built site and tell us what you'd like adjusted. We work through the agreed revision rounds until everything is exactly as it should be.

06

Launch & Handover

We deploy the site, set up the domain, and make sure everything is live and working. You get the full source code, a walkthrough of the site, and a support window to handle anything post-launch.

// faq

How long does a website take to build?

Starter sites take around 1 week, Standard 2–3 weeks, and Super timelines are agreed per project. Content readiness and revision rounds can affect delivery.

Do I need to provide the content?

Yes. You provide your text, images, and branding assets. We can advise on structure and sourcing. Copywriting is available as a paid add-on.

Can you update my existing site?

Yes. Share details about your current site and what you'd like changed and we'll assess and quote from there.

What do I need to get started?

Just a rough idea of what you want, some sites you like the look of, and your budget. We handle the rest from the discovery call onwards.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes. Post-launch support is available for updates, fixes, and small changes, either case-by-case or as a retainer. Discuss this during your discovery call.
