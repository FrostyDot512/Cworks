03Process

How We
Work

A clear, collaborative workflow from first conversation to launch day.

01

Discovery

We understand your goals, your audience, and your vision. Every project starts here — no assumptions, no pressure.

02

Proposal

Clear scope, timeline, and cost. No hidden fees. We agree on everything — 50% deposit to start, balance on launch.

03

Design

We build the visual direction in Figma or directly in-browser. You review and approve the layout before we write any code.

04

Build

Clean HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No bloated frameworks unless needed. We keep you updated throughout with regular previews.

05

Revisions

You review a live staging version. We refine until everything is exactly right within your included revision rounds.

06

Launch

We go live, connect your domain and hosting, and hand over full source code with a walkthrough. You own everything.

// FAQ

How long does a website take to build?

Most projects are completed in 1–3 weeks. Starter sites typically take around 1 week; more complex builds take 2–3 weeks depending on scope.

Do I need to provide the content?

You provide the text, images, and any branding you have. We can help with placeholder content and advise on structure if needed.

Can you update my existing site?

Yes — we work with existing HTML/CSS sites. Get in touch with details and we'll give you a quote for the updates needed.

What do I need to get started?

Just fill in the form or message us. We'll set up a brief call, send a proposal, and once agreed — you pay 50% and we begin.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes. After the free 30-day post-launch support, we offer monthly maintenance plans for updates, backups, and minor changes.
