About Us

We Build.
We Ship.
We Grow.

Cworks (Code Works) is a Ugandan team of developers delivering real digital solutions — from building responsive websites and designing clean user interfaces to automating workflows and building custom systems.

We began as a group of learners, and that spirit still defines how we work: with honesty, teamwork, creativity, and genuine care for every project we ship.

We understand the needs of local businesses, students, startups, and growing brands because we come from the same environment. Whether you're running a small business in Kampala, building a personal brand, or bringing a new idea to life — Cworks is the team to build it with you.
