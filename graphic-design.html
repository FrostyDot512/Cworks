// how it works

Our Process

A clear, collaborative design workflow from first brief to final files.

01

Brief & Discovery

We start by understanding your brand, your audience, and what you want people to feel. The more context you share upfront, the better the output. We ask the right questions to make sure we're solving the right problem.

02

Research & Moodboard

We look at your industry, your competitors, and the visual references that resonate with you. We put together a moodboard — a collection of colours, styles, and directions — and align with you before any design work begins.

03

Concepts

We present initial design concepts for your review. For logos, this typically means two to three distinct directions. For other deliverables, we share a first draft and walk you through the reasoning behind the choices.

04

Refinement

You choose a direction and give feedback. We refine the design through the agreed number of revision rounds, tightening every detail until the result is exactly what you need.

05

Delivery

We deliver the final files in all formats you need — print-ready and web-ready. We walk you through the files and make sure you know how to use them before we close the project.

// faq

What file formats will I receive?

You'll receive vector files (SVG, PDF) for print and web-optimised exports (PNG, JPG) for digital use. All files are clearly named and organised.

What tools do you use?

We design in Figma. If you need editable source files in another format, let us know and we'll discuss what's possible.

Can you match my existing brand?

Yes. Share your existing brand guidelines, colours, and fonts and we'll design within them. If you don't have guidelines yet, we can help establish them as part of the project.

Do you design for print and digital?

Yes. We handle both. Print files are delivered at the correct resolution and colour profile (CMYK where needed). Digital exports are optimised for web and screen.

How many revision rounds are included?

The number of revision rounds depends on your tier. Additional rounds beyond what's included can be arranged for an extra fee.
